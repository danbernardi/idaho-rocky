The Ranch has 21 comfortable accommodations in log cabins and the historic main lodge, with handcrafted furnishings and private baths.

Cabins
The eight duplex cabins, and Honeymoon Cabin, have rock fireplaces, either Oakley stone showers or deep, relaxing tubs with showers, and King or twin beds. A third person can be accommodated on a rollaway bed. For chilly mornings and evenings, they also have electric heat. Several of our duplex cabins have an interior connecting door for families or groups of four to six people. The Honeymoon and Deluxe Duplex Cabin are our deluxe accomodations. The Honeymoon Cabin is a stand alone structure, providing additional privacy, and the Deluxe Duplex Cabin offers a more spacious design.

Lodge Rooms
Each of the four lodge rooms has a queen bed and Oakley stone shower. The comfortable and convenient lodge rooms are located on a hallway off the main lodge, allowing guests to enjoy the lodge’s large rock fireplace. The rooms have electric heat.

There is a complimentary guest laundry facility on the Ranch. There are no telephones, televisions or radios in the guest rooms. A courtesy phone, pay phone, internet access and other office services are available. View the cabin map to see the layout of our cabins.

Rate Details
Rates are subject to a 15% service charge, applicable state taxes and change without notice.

There is a three night minimum stay on reservations made prior to June 1st, unless we are able to fit your request between existing reservations.

You may place your name on a waitlist for a shorter stay. Beginning June 1st reservations of any length stay are made.

Reservation Deposits & Payments
A 60% deposit is required to confirm all reservations. The balance is due at departure. We accept Visa and Mastercard, personal checks, traveler's checks and money orders.

Cancellation Policy
If reservations are cancelled within 60 days of your scheduled arrival date, you will be held responsible for the total lodging cost (including taxes) if we are not able to rebook the accommodation. If the accommodation is rebooked or you cancel prior to 60 days from your arrival date, a refund will be given, less a cancellation fee of 10% of the total lodging cost plus taxes.
