The Idaho Rocky Mountain Ranch cabins and lodge rooms are charming and comfortable, with private baths with either stone showers or deep, relaxing bathtubs.

Fourteen cabins, our Family Cabin, the Honeymoon Cabin and four lodge rooms provide you a home away from home in which to relax and prepare for your next adventure. And there are no phones, televisions or radios in the accommodations allowing you to truly unplug.

Cabins
Each cabin is well appointed with upgraded (and raved about!) beds and bedding, a rock fireplace, electric heat, private bath and bed(s) for two, either twin beds or a king. A third person can be accommodated on a rollaway bed. Our Cabins are arranged in seven two-cabin duplexes. Four duplexes offer interior connecting doors between the two cabins, to comfortably accommodate families of 4 to 6 people.

Our Deluxe Family Cabin, for larger families, includes two cabins, each with king bed, and a third room with twin beds. The Deluxe Honeymoon Cabin, with king bed, stands alone.

View the cabin layout map.

Lodge Rooms
Each of our 4 attractively decorated lodge rooms has a queen bed, unique Oakley stone shower and electric heat. These rooms are located off the hallway next to the lodge great room.

There is a complimentary guest laundry facility on the Ranch, and a courtesy phone, guest laptop computer and wi-fi in the Lodge Great Room.

Rate Details
Rates are subject to a 15% service charge, applicable state taxes and change without notice.
There is a three night minimum stay on reservations made prior to June 1st, unless we are able to fit your request between existing reservations.
You may place your name on a waitlist for a shorter stay. Beginning June 1st reservations of any length stay are made.

Reservation Deposits & Payments
A 60% deposit is required to confirm all reservations. The balance is due at departure. We accept Visa and Mastercard, personal checks, traveler's checks and money orders.

Cancellation Policy
Your deposit, less a fee of 10% of the total reservation cost, will be refunded if cancellation is made at least 60 days prior to scheduled arrival. If cancellation or early departure notice is given within 60 days of scheduled arrival, you are responsible for the total reservation cost unless we are able to re-book the accommodations. A refund will be given for rebooked accommodations, less a fee of 10% of the total reservation cost.
