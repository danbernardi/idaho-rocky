HISTORIC CHARM MODERN COMFORT

Nestled in the spectacular Sawtooth Mountains, amidst snowcapped peaks and the meandering Salmon River, high alpine adventure and backcountry relaxation await.

For generations people have been discovering the Idaho Rocky Mountain Ranch and returning as family. We invite you to discover us this year.

Our Summer Showcase Series, new this year, features speakers, entertainers, and activities in a variety of topic areas. June events offer photography, fishing, and Idaho wineries. July and August events feature literature, food and wine, and music.

Fill your days hiking, horseback riding, mountain biking, fishing, whitewater rafting, and swimming in the natural hot springs pool. You’re free to create your own unique western adventures with the assistance of our gracious and knowledgeable staff, or to take advantage of a broad array of locally-offered guided activities.
