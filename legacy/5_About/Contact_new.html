Location & Travel
The ranch is located 9 miles South of Stanley, Idaho on Highway 75 between milepost markers 180 and 181. In the summer look for the large entrance sign on the east side; the winter office location is across the highway.

The Ranch is located on the route of the Salmon River - Sawtooth Scenic Byways, one of the Top 10 Drives in the Northern Rockies

The IRMR is located in the Sawtooth Valley 6,600 feet above sea level. For those cool mornings and crisp evenings, we recommend an appropriate cold weather jacket.

Guests who are not local typically fly into Boise, which is a scenic three-hour drive from the Ranch. Alternatively, the Hailey airport which services nearby Sun Valley is an hour and a half by car. The route to the Ranch from Sun Valley airport is over the Galena Summit, providing spectacular views of the Sawtooth Valley.
